## Key Discoveries & Ideas extracted
- **Tenses (Grammar 1-6)**: TOEIC focuses heavily on "signal words" (e.g., *at the moment, yet, already, ago, since, by the time*).
- **Structures (Grammar 7-14)**: Adjective/adverb positions, comparative patterns, gerund vs. infinitive triggers, relative pronouns (who/whom/whose/which/that), passive voice templates, conjunction triggers, and conditional types (including inversion/đảo ngữ).
- **Listening Traps (Listening 1-15)**:
  - Part 1: Similar sounding words, incorrect verbs, wrong positioning of objects.
  - Part 2: Distractors (words in question repeated in wrong context), yes/no response to Wh-questions, tense mismatches.
  - Part 3/4: Paraphrasing (words spoken vs. words in answer choice), numbers/dates traps, global vs. local detail questions.
