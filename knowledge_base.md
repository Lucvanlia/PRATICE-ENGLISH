# Knowledge Base

## Curriculum Outline (Scraped TOEIC Course Rebuild)

### Grammar Units
1. **Present Tenses**: Simple Present & Present Continuous
2. **Present Perfect Tenses**: Present Perfect & Present Perfect Continuous
3. **Past Tenses**: Simple Past & Past Continuous
4. **Past Perfect Tenses**: Past Perfect & Past Perfect Continuous
5. **Future Tenses**: Simple Future & Near Future (Be going to)
6. **Advanced Future**: Future Continuous & Future Perfect
7. **Adjectives & Adverbs**: Position, classification, functions
8. **Comparisons**: Equal, comparative, superlative, and double comparison
9. **Verb Patterns**: Gerund & Infinitive
10. **Clauses**: Relative Clause (and reduced relative clauses)
11. **Passive Voice**: Formulas and special passive structures
12. **Pronouns**: Indefinite Pronouns
13. **Conjunctions**: Coordinated, correlative, and subordinating conjunctions
14. **Conditionals**: Zero, 1st, 2nd, 3rd, mixed conditionals, and inversion

### Listening Units
1. **Part 1**: Picture Description - Objects/Scenes
2. **Part 2**: Picture Description - People/Actions
3. **Part 3**: Question-Response - Where questions
4. **Part 4**: Question-Response - When questions
5. **Part 5**: Question-Response - Who questions
6. **Part 6**: Question-Response - How questions
7. **Part 7**: Question-Response - Why questions
8. **Part 8**: Question-Response - Yes/No questions
9. **Part 9**: Question-Response - Choice questions
10. **Part 10**: Question-Response - Statement questions
11. **Part 11**: Short Conversation - Structure & Tips
12. **Part 12**: Short Talk - Public Announcements
13. **Part 13**: Short Talk - News Reports
14. **Part 14**: Short Talk - Work Announcements
15. **Part 15**: Short Talk - Reports & Charts

## Spaced Repetition Intervals
- Day 1, Day 3, Day 7, Day 14, Day 30
- Flashcards will cover: Vocabularies, grammar rules, listening traps.
