### Indefinite Pronoun Reference Table

| Prefix | Person | Thing | Place |
|--------|--------|-------|-------|
| **every-** | everyone/everybody | everything | everywhere |
| **some-** | someone/somebody | something | somewhere |
| **any-** | anyone/anybody | anything | anywhere |
| **no-** | no one/nobody | nothing | nowhere |

### Subject-Verb Agreement Rules
All indefinite pronouns above are **grammatically singular**:
> **Everyone is** attending. ✓ (not 'are')
> **Something was** wrong. ✓ (not 'were')

### No-Forms with Affirmative Verbs
No-form pronouns already carry negative meaning — adding a negative verb creates a double negative:
> **Nobody knows** the answer. ✓
> ~~Nobody doesn't know the answer.~~ ✗ (double negative)
