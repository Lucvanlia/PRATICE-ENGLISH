Determiners and quantifiers are heavily tested in Part 5. Identifying the number (singular vs. plural) of the noun after the blank is the fastest way to get the correct answer.

### 1. Singular Quantifiers
These determiners must be followed by singular countable nouns:
* **Each**: Emphasizes individual members of a group separately.
* **Every**: Considers all members as a group (similar to *all* but singular).
* **Another**: Means "one more" or "an alternative one."

### 2. Plural Quantifiers
These determiners modify plural or uncountable nouns:
* **All**: Refers to the total number of things.
* **Both**: Refers to exactly two entities.
* **Other**: Refers to alternative things or people.
* **Several / Many / Few**: Quantify multiple items.
