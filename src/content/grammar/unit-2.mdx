Present Perfect Continuous is one of the most tested tense forms in TOEIC Part 5 and 6 (Grammar Fill-in). It emphasises an action that has been happening continuously from the past up to the present moment.

### Key Concept: Duration vs. Result

| Aspect | Tense | Example |
|--------|-------|---------|
| Duration / ongoing | **Present Perfect Continuous** | She has been training employees all month. |
| Result / completion | **Present Perfect** | She has trained 20 employees this month. |

### Signal Words
The two most important signal words are:
* **for** → indicates duration: *for three hours, for two years*
* **since** → indicates starting point: *since 2019, since last Monday*

Additional signals:
* all morning / all day long / all afternoon
* How long...? questions always require PPC answer.
