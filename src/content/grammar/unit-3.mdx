### When + Interruption Pattern
The most common TOEIC grammar test for Past Simple + Past Continuous:

**Long action** (Past Continuous) + **when** + **short interruption** (Past Simple):
> She **was reviewing** the report **when** the client **called**.

**Short event** (Past Simple) + **while** + **Long background** (Past Continuous):
> He **spilled** his coffee **while** she **was presenting** the results.

### Two Simultaneous Actions (While)
When two ongoing actions happen at the same time:
> **While** the team **was preparing** the presentation, the manager **was meeting** with stakeholders.
