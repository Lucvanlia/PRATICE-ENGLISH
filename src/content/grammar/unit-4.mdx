### Two Past Events: Which Comes First?
When two past actions are mentioned, the **earlier** one uses Past Perfect:

| Order | Tense | Example |
|-------|-------|---------|
| Earlier action | **Past Perfect** (had + V3) | She had submitted the report... |
| Later action | **Past Simple** | ...before the deadline passed. |

### Timeline Markers
* **By the time**: Always followed by Past Simple + Past Perfect in main clause.
  > By the time he arrived (PS), she had left (PP).
* **Before / After**: Sequence two events.
  > After they had signed the contract (PP), negotiations began (PS).
