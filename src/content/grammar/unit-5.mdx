### Three Future Forms Compared

| Form | Use | Signal |
|------|-----|--------|
| **will** | Spontaneous decision, promise, general prediction | I think, maybe, probably |
| **be going to** | Pre-planned intention, evidence-based prediction | plan, intend, next + time |
| **Present Simple** | Fixed timetable event | schedule, timetable |

### TOEIC Part 5-6 Focus
TOEIC often tests whether to use 'will' or 'be going to' by providing:
* Context clue about whether a decision was pre-planned or just made.
* Evidence in the sentence that hints at current visible circumstances.
