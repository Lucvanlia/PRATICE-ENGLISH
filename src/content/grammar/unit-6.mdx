Advanced future tenses allow you to express precise scheduling and project milestones.

### 1. Future Continuous (Actions In Progress)
Use this tense to express an action that will be in progress at a specific time in the future:
* **Specific Future Moment**: Highlights what will be happening at a exact hour or date.
* **Interrupted Future Actions**: Long action (Future Continuous) interrupted by a short action (Present Simple).

### 2. Future Perfect (Milestones & Deadlines)
Use this tense to indicate that an action will be completed before a certain point in the future:
* **Deadlines**: Actions finished *before* a future limit (introduced by *by* or *by the time*).
* **Future Duration**: Expressing how long an state will have existed up to a future point.
