Understanding word forms is one of the most effective ways to boost your score in Part 5 of the TOEIC test, as it accounts for about 20-30% of grammar questions.

### 1. Noun (N) Positions
Nouns are placed in the following slots:
* **Subject**: The starting noun before the verb.
* **Object**: After transitive verbs or prepositions.
* **After Adjectives**: Follows the structure: `Adjective + Noun`.

### 2. Adjective (Adj) & Adverb (Adv) Positions
* **Adjective**: Placed before nouns to modify them, or after linking verbs like *be, seem, look, become, feel*.
* **Adverb**: Used to modify verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs. It can be placed at the beginning or end of clauses.
