### Adjective Comparison Rules

| Syllables | Comparative | Superlative |
|-----------|------------|-------------|
| 1 syllable | tall → **taller** | tall → **the tallest** |
| 2 syllables (y ending) | busy → **busier** | busy → **the busiest** |
| 2+ syllables | careful → **more careful** | careful → **the most careful** |
| Irregular | good → **better** | good → **the best** |

### Equality & Inequality Comparisons
* **As...as**: "This report is **as detailed as** the previous one." (equal)
* **Not as...as**: "This version is **not as reliable as** the old one." (inequality)
* **The + comparative, the + comparative**: "**The more** you practise, **the better** you get."
