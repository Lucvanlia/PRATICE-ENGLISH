Verb complementation (choosing V-ing or to-V) is highly repetitive in Part 5 of the TOEIC test. Memorizing verb patterns and preposition patterns is key.

### 1. The Gerund (V-ing)
Used in the following cases:
* **Subject/Object**: Serves as a noun.
* **After Prepositions**: `preposition + V-ing`.
* **Common verbs**: *consider, avoid, suggest, postpone, practice, recommend, enjoy*.

### 2. The Infinitive (to-V)
Used in the following cases:
* **Purpose**: Meaning "in order to."
* **Common verbs**: *agree, refuse, decide, fail, demand, plan, offer, hope*.
