Phone messages and announcements are the two most common Part 4 formats and account for roughly 40-50% of all Part 4 questions.

### 1. Phone Messages (Voicemail Format)
Every voicemail follows a predictable structure:
1. **Identity**: "This is [Name] from [Company]."
2. **Purpose**: "I'm calling to/about..."
3. **Request**: "Please call me back / Send the file / Let me know if..."
4. **Contact**: A number or email address at the end.

### 2. Announcements
Announcements typically inform a specific audience of a change:
1. **Audience**: "Attention all [passengers/employees/shoppers]..."
2. **Change or Event**: What is happening, what has changed.
3. **Action**: What listeners should do in response.
