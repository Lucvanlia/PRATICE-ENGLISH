Advertisements and tour guides are two distinct but frequently appearing Part 4 formats. Each has a unique structure and set of predictable question types.

### 1. Advertisements
Ads are designed to persuade, so they use highly enthusiastic, positive language:
* **Hook**: Opens with a question targeting a pain point.
* **Solution**: Introduces the product or service.
* **Benefits**: Lists key features or advantages.
* **CTA (Call-to-Action)**: Ends with a specific instruction (call, visit, sign up).

### 2. Tour Guide Talks
Tour guides are descriptive and sequential. Listen for:
* **Directional cues**: Left, right, ahead, behind.
* **Historical facts**: Dates, founding years, notable events.
* **Rules/instructions**: What visitors can or cannot do.
