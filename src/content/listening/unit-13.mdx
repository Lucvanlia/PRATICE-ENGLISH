Vocabulary is the backbone of TOEIC performance. For Listening, the most critical vocabulary clusters are action verbs, workplace setting nouns, and spatial prepositions.

### 1. Core Action Verb Groups

| Category | Verbs |
|----------|-------|
| Office | typing, reviewing, signing, filing, presenting, stapling |
| Meeting | discussing, pointing, listening, taking notes, gesturing |
| Movement | walking, carrying, pushing, pulling, climbing |
| Outdoor/Industry | loading, unloading, stacking, assembling, operating |

### 2. Setting Vocabulary

| Indoor Settings | Outdoor Settings |
|-----------------|-----------------|
| conference room | parking lot |
| reception / lobby | construction site |
| storage room / archive | street / sidewalk |
| warehouse floor | train platform / airport |

### 3. Position Prepositions
* **At**: at the desk, at the counter, at the entrance.
* **On**: on the shelf, on the floor, on a ladder.
* **In**: in the hallway, in the warehouse, in a vehicle.
* **In front of / behind / next to / between**: for relative positions.
