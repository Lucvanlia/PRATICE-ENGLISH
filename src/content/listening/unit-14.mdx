Even with strong vocabulary, test-takers lose points by falling into deliberate TOEIC traps. This unit teaches you to recognize and avoid all 5 common trap types.

### 1. The 5 TOEIC Listening Traps

| Trap Type | Mechanism | Defense |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| **Repetition** | Answer repeats audio words with altered meaning | Verify context, not just vocabulary |
| **Correction** | Speaker corrects first statement | Track: "actually," "not," "but" |
| **Similar Sound** | Two words sound alike (sale/sail, right/write) | Focus on meaning, not sound |
| **Irrelevant Truth** | Answer is factually true but doesn't answer the question | Check exactly what the question asks |
| **Negative** | "Not," "never," or "no longer" reverses meaning | Listen carefully for negation |

### 2. Speed Training Tips
* **Shadow listening**: Repeat what you hear at the same time as the audio.
* **Chunked listening**: Practise listening to 5-second audio clips and summarising.
* **Paraphrase training**: When you hear an answer, rephrase it in your own words to test understanding.
