### 1. Exam-Day Part-by-Part Strategy

| Part | Focus | Key Strategy |
|------|-------|-------------|
| Part 1 | Photo description | SWCO scan before audio |
| Part 2 | Question-Response | Identify question type in first 2 seconds |
| Part 3 | Conversations | Read all 3 questions before audio |
| Part 4 | Monologues | Identify talk format in first 10 seconds |

**Week 2: Intermediate**

**Week 4: Full Tests**
* Days 22-25: Full Part 3 practice + error review
* Days 26-28: Full Part 4 practice + error review
* Days 29-30: Complete TOEIC listening mock exam + analysis
