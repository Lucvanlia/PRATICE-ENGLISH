Part 1 of the TOEIC Listening test features 6 photographs. Roughly 70-80% of them contain people. Your job is to select the sentence that most accurately describes what you see.

### 1. People-Focused Photo Analysis
When observing the photograph before audio plays, use the **SWCO scan**:
* **S – Subject**: Who is in the picture? One person, two, or a group?
* **W – What**: What is the main ongoing action?
* **C – Context**: Indoors or outdoors? Office, factory, restaurant?
* **O – Object**: What object is being used, held, or operated?

### 2. Common Traps to Avoid
* **Wrong count**: Photo has 1 person, but choice says "two employees" → Eliminate.
* **Wrong action**: Photo shows someone reading, but choice says "writing" → Eliminate.
* **Being + V3 trap**: "The desk is being cleaned" when no one is cleaning → Eliminate.
