WHERE questions appear 3-4 times in Part 2 (Questions 7–31). They test your ability to link spatial information to a relevant response.

### 1. Structure of WHERE Questions
WHERE questions in TOEIC can take many forms:
* **Direct**: "Where is the presentation room?"
* **Embedded**: "Do you know where the annual report is filed?"
* **Indirect**: "Where should we hold the team-building event?"

### 2. Response Strategies
* **Match the category**: The response must be location-based. Time, reason, or Yes/No responses are wrong.
* **Indirect redirects are valid**: If the answer names a person or department responsible for the location, it is still a correct response.
