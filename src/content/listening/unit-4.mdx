WHEN questions are the second most common Part 2 question type after general questions. They test your ability to distinguish time-based responses from other categories.

### 1. WHEN Question Patterns
* **Simple**: "When is the board meeting?"
* **Future**: "When will the shipment arrive?"
* **Embedded**: "Do you know when the renovation will finish?"

### 2. Response Strategy
Focus on time vocabulary when listening to options:
* **Accept**: Day, date, month, period, "soon," "shortly," "later today."
* **Reject immediately**: Location answers, Yes/No responses, reason-based answers.
