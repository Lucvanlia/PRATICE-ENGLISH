WHAT questions are the most diverse type in Part 2. HOW questions are more structured with clear sub-types. Identifying the sub-type is key to finding the matching answer quickly.

### 1. WHAT Question Strategies
WHAT questions demand substantive information (facts, plans, objects, names, advice):
* **WHAT + time** = answer with a time expression (treated like WHEN).
* **WHAT + kind/type** = answer with a description or category.
* **WHAT + should I / can we** = answer with advice, action, or suggestions.

### 2. HOW Question Strategies
HOW questions are easy to categorize by the following word:
* **HOW + much/many** → quantity or price.
* **HOW + long** → duration.
* **HOW + often** → frequency.
* **HOW + do/can** → method or process.
