WHO questions in Part 2 test your ability to match the identity of a person or role to the action described in the question.

### 1. WHO Question Variants
WHO questions appear in several forms:
* **Direct**: "Who is managing the renovation project?"
* **Embedded**: "Do you know who approved these travel expenses?"
* **Indirect**: "Who should I contact about the software licence?"

### 2. Response Strategy
Focus on person-identification vocabulary when listening:
* **Accept**: Names, department names, job titles, indirect person references.
* **Reject**: Location phrases, Yes/No answers, time expressions.
