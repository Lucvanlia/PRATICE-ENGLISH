Part 3 is the longest part of the TOEIC Listening section and requires strategic multi-tasking: reading ahead while simultaneously processing audio.

### 1. The Preview-First Technique
The single most important skill for Part 3 is **reading questions before listening**:
* Use the transition time between questions to read the next set of 3 questions.
* Identify key words and question types to direct your listening focus.

### 2. Common Part 3 Question Types
* **Topic questions**: "What is the conversation mainly about?" → Listen for the shared subject.
* **Detail questions**: "What does the man say about...?" → Focus on specific words.
* **Next action questions**: "What will the woman do next?" → Focus on the ending of the conversation.
* **Graphic questions**: Match chart/schedule data with conversation details.
